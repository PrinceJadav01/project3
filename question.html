The time it takes to sell your home can vary based on factors like location, market conditions, and pricing. On average, homes may sell within 30 to 90 days, but our team at Infinity Real Estate can provide a more tailored estimate based on your specific situation.

The ideal list price for your home should be based on a detailed market analysis that considers recent sales of similar properties in your area, the unique features of your home, and current market trends. At Infinity Real Estate, our team of experts will provide you with a comparative market analysis (CMA) to help you set a competitive and realistic price that attracts buyers while maximizing your return.

The best time to sell a home often depends on market conditions, but generally, spring and early summer are considered ideal. During these months, demand tends to be higher, with more buyers actively searching for homes. Additionally, homes often show better in favorable weather, which can enhance curb appeal. At Infinity Real Estate, we monitor market trends closely and can provide personalized advice on the optimal timing for your specific situation.
